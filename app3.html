Alter Ego

«Well, look who showed up. I grew tired of waiting.» Es in "Self-Discovery Begins" dialogue


Es is a young lady who appears in the game Alter Ego, by Caramel Column.
Her role is to guide the player through self-discovery, while learning about herself in the process.

The gameplay is very similar to a classic Clicker but the atmosphere is different and unique.
We quickly become attached to the games,unlock new dialogues by collecting the game currency, "The ego", obtained by reading new books all having a philosphic appeal.
